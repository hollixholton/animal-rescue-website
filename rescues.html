Why Donate?

Here at Cedar Row, we run on a complete volunteer basis. Since the farm is non-profit, we rely heavily on donations and help from our community to keep afloat. Sometimes medication or unexpected costs pop up and leave us in a tough spot, so every dollar we recieve truly helps to make everyone happy here.
